How to Use:

  • Type messages in the input field and click send or press Enter
  • Messages are automatically saved locally in your browser
  • Chatbot responds to keywords: hello, help, weather, time, name
  • Use "Clear Chat" to remove all messages from storage
  • Messages will persist even after refreshing the page
